Who are we?

The Qarsak team is proud to provide you with a one-stop entertainment solution. We are passionate about serving our community - bringing innovation and efficiency for your ease and keeping you up to date with all Afghan events happening near you or worldwide.


What do we do?

Qarsak.com is an online portal with some of the most advance features that have made buying tickets online easy with simple steps and hassle-free checkout:

  • Browse the upcoming events or enter what you are looking for in the search box.
  • Place your order
  • Receive tickets either via email or text message.

What qarsak.com offers?

We offer an event calendar of events happening worldwide, including paid and free events. Qarsak also offers booking event tickets with your choice of seats (if the event organizers provide the seating option).
